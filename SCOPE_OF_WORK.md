## 1. Project Overview

Vivah Verse is an online platform designed to transform Indian wedding planning by connecting couples with premium venues and vendors. The platform leverages technology and AI to provide venue discovery, booking facilitation, and personalized concierge services, acting as an intermediary between users and service providers.

---

## 2. Theme & Design Philosophy

- **Brand Values:** Love-centered, excellence, community, and trust. Every decision is guided by a desire to help couples celebrate their unique love story.
- **Visual Style:** Modern, elegant, and vibrant. Uses a rich color palette (rani pink, gold, charcoal) and high-quality imagery to evoke celebration and luxury.
- **User Experience:** Mobile-first, intuitive, and welcoming. Smooth animations, clear CTAs, and a focus on reducing planning stress.

---

## 3. How It Works

### Step 1: Onboarding
- Users provide basic wedding details (date, city, guest count, budget) via a guided flow.
- The platform personalizes recommendations and planning tools based on these inputs.

### Step 2: Explore & Compare
- Users browse curated venues and vendors, filter by preferences, and compare options side-by-side.
- Detailed profiles, reviews, and transparent pricing help users make informed decisions.

### Step 3: AI Concierge Assistance
- Users can chat with the AI-powered concierge for personalized suggestions (venues, vendors, packages, dates).
- The concierge extracts user intent and provides actionable recommendations, with direct links to booking or more info.

### Step 4: Build or Choose a Package
- Users select from pre-designed wedding packages or build a custom package (venue, catering, decor, services).
- Real-time price calculation and review before proceeding.

### Step 5: Booking & Management
- Users book venues and services directly through the platform.
- Dashboard provides an overview of bookings, planning status, and next steps.

### Step 6: Inspiration & Community
- Users can browse real weddings for inspiration and ideas.
- Community features and reviews foster trust and engagement.

---

## 4. Core Features

### 4.1 Venue Discovery & Comparison
- Search and filter venues by city, price, capacity, amenities, and type.
- Compare multiple venues side-by-side on key features (price per plate, capacity, rating, amenities, etc.).
- View detailed venue profiles with images, descriptions, and reviews.

### 4.2 Booking Facilitation
- Guided onboarding to collect wedding details (date, guests, city, budget).
- Booking flow to reserve venues and services.
- Dashboard for users to manage their bookings and planning journey.

### 4.3 AI-Powered Concierge
- Chat-based interface to provide personalized recommendations for venues, vendors, dates, and packages.
- AI extracts user intent and preferences (city, date, guests, budget) to suggest optimal options.
- Seamless navigation from recommendations to relevant booking or service pages.

### 4.4 Wedding Packages
- Pre-designed package tiers (Essential, Premium, Luxury) with transparent pricing and inclusions.
- Custom package builder allowing users to select venues, catering, decor, and additional services.
- Real-time price calculation and review before booking.

### 4.5 Vendor & Service Marketplace
- Browse and book vendors (decor, catering, photography, DJ, etc.) by category and city.
- View vendor profiles, specialties, ratings, and starting prices.

### 4.6 Real Weddings & Inspiration
- Showcase of real weddings planned through the platform for inspiration.
- Filters for style, location, and other attributes.

### 4.7 User Accounts & Admin
- User registration, authentication, and profile management.
- Admin dashboard for managing venues, vendors, bookings, and user data.

## 5. Technology Stack

- **Frontend:** Next.js (React), TypeScript, Framer Motion, Lucide Icons, custom UI components.
- **Backend:** NestJS, TypeScript, Prisma ORM, Meilisearch for search, REST/GraphQL APIs.
- **Database:** Managed via Prisma migrations.
- **AI/ML:** Custom recommendation and chat logic for the concierge.
- **DevOps:** Docker, docker-compose for local development and deployment.
